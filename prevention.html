We're Here For You

It takes courage to reach out. These resources are available 24/7 with trained responders who understand and care:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline 988
  • Crisis Text Line Text HOME to 741741
  • Schizophrenia & Psychosis Support 1-800-950-0063

These conversations are confidential, and the person on the other end is trained to help with exactly what you're going through.
